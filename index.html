I am currently a PhD Student at the Secure Systems group at the Institute of Applied Information Processing and Communications at Graz University of Technology. My research interests include side-channel analysis on secure systems, especially power-analysis attacks and countermeasures.

I obtained my Bachelor and Master's degrees in Computer Science as and Software Engineering and Management from Graz University of Technology. A detailed CV can be found here.

Whenever I'm not researching information security, I enjoy creative writing. I write poems, stories and my first novel is under progress. Occasionally, I read my texts at poetry slams and open mics.
