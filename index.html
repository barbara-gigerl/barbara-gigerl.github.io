About

Welcome to my site! :D I finished my PhD in June 2024 in the Secure Systems group at the Institute of Applied Information Processing and Communications at Graz University of Technology. My research interests include side-channel analysis on secure systems, especially power-analysis attacks and countermeasures. I am also interested in verification of hardware designs.

I obtained my Bachelor and Master's degrees in Computer Science as and Software Engineering and Management from Graz University of Technology.
